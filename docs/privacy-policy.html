Privacy Policy for Fusion Grid

Effective date: 2026-03-24

Fusion Grid is an offline puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

1) Information We Collect

Fusion Grid does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2) Data Stored on Your Device

Fusion Grid stores some gameplay-related data locally on your device, including:

  • Best score
  • Game preferences (for example, sound/vibration settings)

This data is stored locally using on-device storage and is not transmitted to our servers by the current version of the app.

3) Network and Third-Party Services

The current version of Fusion Grid is designed to function offline.

If a future version introduces online services, ads, or analytics SDKs, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or at the time those features are released.

4) Children’s Privacy

Fusion Grid is not specifically directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

5) Data Retention

Because gameplay data is stored locally on your device, you can remove it by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

6) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective date at the top of this page.
